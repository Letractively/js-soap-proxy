JS SOAP Proxy samples index

  • BasicService samples BasicService works with XMLSchema primitive types
  • ArraysService samples ArraysService works with XMLSchema Arrays of primitive types
  • ObjectsService samples ArraysService works with XMLSchema Structs of primitive types
  • MixedService samples MixedService works with XMLSchema Arrays of XMLSchema Structs, XMLSchema Structs with XMLSchema Arrays, XMLSchema Structs in XMLSchema Structs and so on.
  • WSDLFormats samples WSDLFormatsService works with different formats of SOAP definitions
